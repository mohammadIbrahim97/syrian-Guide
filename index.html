Evolved from WithLocals brand DNA. Built for Syrian Campus Guides.

Design Principles

SyriaGuide connects international travelers with local university student guides. The UI architecture mirrors WithLocals' warm, pill-shaped design system while implementing refined color choices representing Syrian heritage.

Warm Syrian Hospitality

We use curved boundaries, soft shadows, and warm cream palettes to convey the cultural hospitality ("Ahlan wa Sahlan") of Damascus and Aleppo.

Education & Trust

Guides are verified university students. The design showcases badges representing certifications, campus ratings, and language fluencies clearly.

Accessible & Multilingual

Dynamic alignment adjustments ensure headings, text fields, and ratings remain visually balanced in both English (LTR) and Arabic (RTL).

Color Palette

Tokens reflecting the terracotta bricks of Syrian cities, the cobalt tiles of historic mosques, and sandstone columns.

Syrian Amber

#FF5E3A

Primary brand token. Used for critical CTAs, book buttons, and accent rings. Evolved from WithLocals' primary orange.

Damascus Blue

#1A365D

Deep indigo. Used for headings, nav elements, footer background, and trust metrics. Establishes brand stability.

Desert Gold

#DFB26C

Warm yellow gold. Used for review stars, premium tier indicators, and highlighted tag borders.

Night Sky

#1E1E24

Off-black charcoal. Primary text color, ensuring high legibility and contrast ratio on light backgrounds.

Limestone

#FBF9F6

Off-white background cream. Gives pages a soft, organic texture instead of stark blinding white.

Pure White

#FFFFFF

Surface containers, profile cards, input backgrounds, and navigation wraps.

Typography

We pair Plus Jakarta Sans (humanist display sans-serif) with Inter (highly legible body text) and Noto Sans Arabic.
